Have a question? Check out the FAQ

Welcome to the FAQ section of Idiot Activities Club! We're here to answer your most common questions about discovering, organizing, and participating in community events through our app.

What is the Idiot Activities Club app?

The Idiot Activities Club is a platform designed to help users discover, register, and participate in events and activities hosted by clubs and organizations in their community.

How do I register for an event?

Simply browse the available events, select the one you're interested in, and tap the "Register" button. You'll receive reminders and updates for registered events.

How do I create an event as a club leader?

Club leaders can log in to their accounts, navigate to the "Create Event" section, and fill out the event details, including time, date, location, and description.

How does the app ensure data security?

We prioritize your privacy and security with encrypted data storage, secure user authentication, and regular security updates.

Is the app free to use?

Yes, the Idiot Activities Club app is free for users, with optional premium features for clubs and organizations.

How do admins approve or manage events?

Admins can log in to their dashboard, review submitted events, and approve or reject them based on the platform's guidelines. They can also manage existing events and monitor user activity.
